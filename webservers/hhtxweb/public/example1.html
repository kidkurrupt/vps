Meet Your Therapist

Hello, I'm Natalie Parffrey. I am a Licensed Practicing Counselor Associate. My goal is to provide you with the support and tools to overcome life’s challenges. Let’s work together to create a path toward your personal healing and growth.

Despite what ole Forrest Gump shared with us while sitting on a park bench, life may not always be a box of chocolates. Sometimes it just seems like a downright box of rocks. Sometimes, we might feel overwhelmed, stuck, irritated, anxious, depressed, alone, misunderstood, and frustrated with the way things are and the burdens we carry. We may struggle to understand ourselves and those we love, or perhaps we think something must be wrong with us or our world. You might wonder what is the purpose of all of this and what am I supposed to do! How can I find happiness and peace?

Let’s Start With Putting Down That Box Of Rocks.

As an experienced solution-focused and trauma-informed therapist, I believe the answers you are seeking are closer than they appear. I believe in a tailored approach for my clients that will aid you in bringing your subconscious wisdom to the forefront of your mind.

You don’t have to do this alone. There are answers, and I am prepared to help you explore these questions. Therapy is a place where, together, we can navigate your thoughts, feelings, and subconscious mind while tapping into your strengths, to aid you in discovering the peace you are seeking in your mind, body, and relationship.

Experience and Trainings: Trauma informed and Trained in EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization Reprocessing), CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy), DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy), Gottman Relationship Counseling, Relational Therapy, Hypnotherapy.

Member of the International Honors Society in Psychology, 4.0 GPA. Family and Individual Lifespan Development, Positive Psychology, Attachment-Based Practice, Early Childhood Education and Development, BA Psychology, AAS Early Childhood Development.

Our Care Options

  • Individual Counseling

    Personalized counseling sessions focused on your unique challenges and goals.

  • Couples Therapy

    Improve communication and strengthen your relationship with guided therapy sessions. Trained in the Gottman and RLT methods.

  • Trauma Therapy

    Work through past trauma with evidence-based techniques and compassionate support.

  • Hypnotherapy

    Guided relaxation and focused attention to achieve a heightened state of awareness, helping individuals access and address subconscious thoughts and behaviors.

  • EMDR Therapy

    Designed to help individuals process and heal from trauma by using guided eye movements to reduce the emotional intensity of distressing memories.

  • Online Therapy

    Convenient and effective virtual therapy sessions from the comfort of your own space using a secure platform.
