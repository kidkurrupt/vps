Our Care Options

  • Individual counseling offers a private, confidential space to explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors with a trained professional. This one-on-one therapy is tailored to your specific needs, whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, life transitions, or seeking personal growth. Together, we will set goals and develop coping strategies to help you navigate challenges, improve your well-being, and build a more fulfilling life.

  • Couples therapy provides a supportive environment for you and your partner to address challenges and strengthen your bond. Using proven methods like the Gottman Method and Relational Life Therapy (RLT), we focus on improving communication, resolving conflicts constructively, and deepening emotional intimacy. The goal is to equip you with the tools to navigate disagreements, rebuild trust, and foster a healthier, more resilient partnership.

  • Trauma therapy is a specialized approach designed to help individuals heal from the emotional and psychological effects of distressing life events. In a safe and empathetic setting, we use evidence-based techniques to help you process traumatic memories and reduce symptoms such as anxiety, flashbacks, and emotional numbness. Our aim is to help you regain a sense of safety, control, and peace in your life.

  • Hypnotherapy is a therapeutic technique that uses guided relaxation and intense concentration to achieve a heightened state of awareness, often called a trance. While in this state, you are more open to suggestions that can help you modify perceptions, behaviors, and emotions. It is a powerful tool for addressing issues like phobias, anxiety, and unwanted habits by accessing the subconscious mind to create positive, lasting change.

  • Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is a highly effective therapy for overcoming trauma. It works by using bilateral stimulation, such as guided eye movements, to help the brain reprocess traumatic memories. This process allows the emotional distress associated with the memory to be resolved, enabling you to recall the event without the intense, painful feelings, and to move forward with your life.

  • Online therapy, or telehealth, offers the same professional support as in-person sessions but with the added convenience and comfort of being in your own space. Using a secure and confidential video platform, you can access high-quality therapy from anywhere with an internet connection. This flexible option makes it easier to fit counseling into your busy schedule without sacrificing the effectiveness of the therapeutic process.
