Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Torch Card

What makes Torch Card different from other digital business cards?
Torch Card combines beautiful design with powerful QR technology to create a seamless networking experience. Unlike other solutions, we focus on both aesthetics and functionality, ensuring your digital card makes a lasting impression while being incredibly easy to use.
How do I share my Torch Card?
You can share your Torch Card in multiple ways: scan your QR code, share a direct link, or even set it as your phone's lock screen for instant sharing. All methods instantly save your contact information to the recipient's phone.
Can I update my information after creating my Torch Card?
Yes! One of the biggest advantages of Torch Card is that you can update your information anytime. All your existing QR codes and links will automatically show your updated information - no need to reprint or redistribute anything.
Do recipients need to install an app to save my contact information?
No, recipients don't need any special app. When they scan your QR code or visit your Torch Card link, their phone's native contact saving functionality is used - it works on all modern smartphones.
How long does it take to get my Torch Card?
After purchasing, you'll work with our design team to create your perfect card. The process typically takes 2-3 business days depending on the complexity of your design and our current workload.
Can I use my own QR code design?
Absolutely! While we provide professional QR code designs as part of our service, you're welcome to use your own custom QR code design if you prefer.
What happens if I lose my phone with my QR code?
No problem! Your Torch Card is stored in our system. You can always log in to your account to retrieve your QR code or have us send you a new one.
Can I track who views my Torch Card?
Our Torch plan includes basic analytics that show you how many times your card has been viewed. For more detailed analytics, please contact us about our enterprise solutions.
Is there a limit to how many times my Torch Card can be shared?
No! Your Torch Card can be shared an unlimited number of times - it truly never "burns out." This is one of the key advantages over traditional paper business cards.
What if I need help with my Torch Card?
We offer excellent customer support for all our Torch Card users. You can reach our support team via email at support@torchcard.com, and we typically respond within 24 hours.
